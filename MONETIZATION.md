# Deckd Monetization — Lazy Growth Path

## Source
Based on @athcanft's "$0 → $10K MRR in 1 month" guide shared by @kr0der.

## Core Philosophy
Not for high-effort UGC army builders. The lazy way:
1. Spin up the app
2. Find a winning ad
3. Scale the hell out of it

## Key Principles

### Minimal UGC
- Don't warm up 20 accounts
- Don't message 50 influencers
- One person can do this

### Win Fast, Scale Fast
- Get the app live
- Run small ad tests (~$50-100 each)
- Find the ad variant with best conversion
- Double down on winner, kill losers

### What's NOT This
- Not organic content growth
- Not community building
- Not influencer outreach
- Not "build it and they will come"

## For Deckd Specifically

### Target Audience
Poker players who want private games with friends — no account needed, BLE-local.

### Growth Loops
1. **Word of mouth** — if it's fun at a party/bar, people share
2. **TikTok/Reels** — viral poker hand videos, card flip animations
3. **Reddit** — r/poker, r/homegames, local poker communities

### Ad Strategy
- Focus on "no account needed" angle vs. Poker Bros
- Show the BLE multiplayer in action
- Target casual poker players, not serious grinders

### Monetization Options
1. **In-app purchases** — card decks, table themes, chip packs
2. **Premium clubs** — hosted games with stakes tracking
3. **Sponsored tables** — poker room advertising
